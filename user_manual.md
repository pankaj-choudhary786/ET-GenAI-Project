## 🤖 Step 2: Testing the 4 Autonomous Agents

NexusAI runs four distinct AI agents. Here is how to trigger and see their output:

### 1. The Prospecting Agent (Lead Gen)
- **Go to**: `App > Prospecting`
- **What it does**: Scans the market for companies matching your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP).
- **How to test**: 
    - Click **"Run Prospecting Agent"**. 
    - You will see new prospects appear with **ICP Scores** (e.g., 92/100) and a **Fit Reason** generated by Claude.
    - Click **"View Sequence"** to see the 3-email outreach plan the agent wrote.

### 2. The Deal Intelligence Agent (Pipeline Risk)
- **Go to**: `App > Pipeline`
- **What it does**: Monitors your CRM for deals that are "stalling" or mentioned a competitor.
- **How to test**:
    - Look for deals with high **Risk Scores** (e.g., Goblin Corp at 85%).
    - Click **"View Recovery Play"**. The agent has analyzed the risk and drafted a "Rescue Email" for you to approve.

### 3. The Retention AI (Churn Detection)
- **Go to**: `App > Retention`
- **What it does**: Analyzes account telemetry (low logins, high support tickets) to predict churn.
- **How to test**:
    - View the **"Risk Heatmap"**.
    - The agent automatically moves at-risk accounts into the **"Intervention Pending"** column and drafts a retention strategy.

### 4. The Competitive Radar (Market Intelligence)
- **Go to**: `App > Battlecards`
- **What it does**: Scrapes the web for competitor news and updates your sales battlecards on the fly.
- **How to test**:
    - View the **Salesforce** or **HubSpot** cards.
    - Check the **"Recent Signals"** section at the bottom to see news headlines scraped by the `scraper.js` service.

## 🛠️ Internal Troubleshooting (For Judges)

### Fixing Google OAuth 401
If you prefer to use your own Google Account:
1. Go to [Google Cloud Console](https://console.cloud.google.com/).
2. Navigate to **APIs & Services > Credentials**.
3. Edit your OAuth 2.0 Client ID.
4. Add **Authorized Redirect URI**: `http://localhost:5000/api/auth/google/callback`.
5. Ensure your email is added to the **OAuth Consent Screen > Test Users** list.

---

## 🛡️ Troubleshooting: Browser Console Errors

### 🚫 "ERR_BLOCKED_BY_CLIENT" (POST play.google.com)
If you see red errors in your browser console mentioning `play.google.com` being blocked:
- **Reason**: You have an **AdBlocker** (uBlock, AdBlock Plus, etc.) active. It is blocking Google's tracking scripts.
- **Fix**: This does **not** affect the app. However, if you want a clean console for the judges, **disable your AdBlocker for `localhost`**.

### 🔑 Fixing "Error 401: invalid_client"
If the **Continue with Google** button fails:
1.  **Check Client ID**: Open your `.env` file and ensure `GOOGLE_CLIENT_ID` has no spaces or quotes. 
2.  **Verify Redirect URI**: In Google Cloud Console, the Redirect URI **MUST** be: `http://localhost:5000/api/auth/google/callback`.
3.  **App Status**: Ensure your Google Project is in "Testing" mode and **your email address** is added to the "Test Users" list.
